About Me

I'm a Masters student with a passion for biological research. My journey began in seconday school, when I learnt that all organisms were encoded by the basic ATGC building blocks, and yet each and every organism can be so very complex and different. It was so interesting to learn how all the components work together to create the infinitesmally complex human body.

In my work, I love learning new things and being at the frontlines of research, exploring areas that were previously unexplored and discovering new facts. I am most comfortable coding in Python. Outside of work and school, I am often either doing some form of art or playing Minecraft.
