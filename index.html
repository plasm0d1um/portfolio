About Me

I'm a Masters student with a passion for biological research. My journey began in seconday school, when I learnt that all organisms are encoded by the fundamental ATGC building blocks. The way these basic components were able to combine and work together to create the incredibly complex and diverse forms of life is fascinating.

In my work, I love learning new things and being at the forefront of research, exploring uncharted territories and uncovering new insights. I am most comfortable coding in Python, which I use often in my projects. Outside of my academic pursuits, I enjoy expressing myself through various forms of art, and immersing myself in the world of Minecraft.
